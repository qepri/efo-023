    In Beijing, China, on the east side of the Tian 'anmen Square, there is a Calamus River Park. Every Tuesday and Saturday, the park becomes a garden of Eden for old men and women to meet each other. Mr. Gao, an 57-year-old bachelor, has insisted on attending this rendezvous for more than 10 years, searching for his ideal lover, or more specifically, his ideal sexual partner. Finally, he met a woman who was willing to go with him, but unfortunately, he messed it up…...
