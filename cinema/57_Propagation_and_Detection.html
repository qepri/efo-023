    single-channel video B/W 16:9 stereo 07 min Stereophonic sound creation by Renato Grieco Italy, 2020 “Propagation and Detection” is a work that investigates the space of an immaterial point. Dissecting an image of the Parthenon and revealing what is hiding in the invisible field, inside the mechanics dispositive, within the wave’s propagation. The visible represents exactly a point, the concrete limit of a distance, which is perceived by human eyes; a recognisable sign that changes into something else, matter changing into an imprint.
