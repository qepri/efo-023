    Sun, light, god, and Hagia Sophia. The symphony between light and nature. . Searching for the ancient sun god in the city of Istanbul. Our city changes as the sunrise. The colors we see are changing. Looking at Istanbul from Hagia Sophia. The sun's rays expand and spread. Looking at the sanctity, everyday life, and the crowd of the city of Istanbul. Sun cleanses our city, our souls, and everything imaginable. Inspired by Thomas Merton's poem "Hagia Sophia".
