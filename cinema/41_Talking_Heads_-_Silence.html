    09:35 min

    My Talking Heads is reinterpretation of Krzysztof Kieslowski documentary movie from 1980. In my movie there are only responses in polish sign language. Deaf people, sign language interpreters and people in this environment answer three questions: How old are you?, Who you are?, What do you want from life?
