• Of Light and of Breath

    • Théo Michel

    (France )

    15:00 min

    The day rises gradually. The sun appears, the forest wakes up. Little by little, the animals appear and the human being invites itself in this park of Ile-de-France. Bodies cross each other, stroll around, in the middle of a sound and light show. Of Light and of Breath takes us into an experience of the forest, a stroll, where the human is no longer the main character.
