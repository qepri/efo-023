    FRAMES FRAGMENTS FREQUENCIES is an experimental short film project in eight chapters. Each chapter creates new insights into diverse in-between worlds. Much of it seems like a cut through different layers of time: an anachronism in which associations simultaneously bring forth the past and the future; prehistory and science fiction at the same time. Between game, ritual, invocation, film noir, and party; poetry and pop shake hands. Each chapter of FRAMES FRAGMENTS FREQUENCIES can be seen as an independent short film.
